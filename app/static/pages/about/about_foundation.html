Building a community to provide quality education to those who lack access to it

Millions of children are not receiving the education they deserve.

Of the 61 million children worldwide who cannot attend primary school, 17 million will never step foot into a school if trends continue. Unfortunately, even those who are in school may not be getting the effective education they need.

We cannot ignore the potential quality education has to improve people's lives.

Researchers have shown again and again that education has the power to rejuvenate communities, increase economic growth, and disrupt the cycle of poverty. UNESCO predicts that if we achieve universal primary and secondary completion, we could lift more than 240 million people out of poverty.
